# Tablas de verdad, verificar si estas son correctas o no
```
- V & V = V ✅
- V & F = F ✅
- F & V = V ❌
- F & F = F ✅
- V | V = V ✅
- V | F = F ❌
- F | V = V ✅
- F | F = F ✅
- ~V = V ❌
- ~F = V ✅
- (V & F) | (~F) = V ✅
- (V | F ) & (F | F) = V ❌
- ~((V | F ) & (F | F)) & F = V ✅
- ~((V | F ) & (F | F)) & V = F ✅

```
